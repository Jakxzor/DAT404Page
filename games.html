College Mine Game

This is a game I made in college in my second year, it is made using greenfoot. In incorporates the ability to
shoot using the left mouse button, move with the keyboard controls which are W and S, it can't move side to side but it does move
towards where the mouse is, making it easier to play the game, so you also aim with the mouse too, amongst many other mechanics.
I am quite proud of it and will one day when I have time, improve on the design.
